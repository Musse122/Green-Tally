Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

When you use our carbon calculator, we may collect:

  • Account info: Your email and name when you sign up
  • Calculator data: Transport, electricity, and diet choices
  • Device info: Browser type and IP address
  • Cookies: Small data files stored on your device

How We Use Your Information

We use this information to:

  • Calculate your carbon footprint
  • Save your results if you create an account
  • Generate tips to reduce your impact
  • Fix bugs and improve our website
  • Send occasional emails about updates (if you opt in)

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you might have the right to:

  • See what data we have about you
  • Correct mistakes in your data
  • Delete your account and data
  • Take your data elsewhere
  • Object to certain uses of your data

Email us if you want to exercise these rights.

Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and keep you logged in. You can set your browser to block cookies, but some parts of our site might not work properly.
