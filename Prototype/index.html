Track Your Carbon Footprint

Our simple carbon calculator helps you see how your daily choices affect the planet.

How to use this calculator:

  1. Enter the distance you travel and your main transport method
  2. Add your monthly electricity usage in kilowatt-hours (kWh)
  3. Select your diet type from the dropdown menu
  4. Indicate how many people live in your household
  5. Click "Calculate Impact" to see your results

How Our Calculator Works

Transportation

We multiply your travel distance by the carbon emission factor for your chosen transport method:

  • Car: 0.404 kg CO₂ per kilometer
  • Bus: 0.089 kg CO₂ per kilometer
  • Train: 0.041 kg CO₂ per kilometer
  • Bike/Walking: 0 kg CO₂

Electricity

Your electricity usage is calculated using an average emissions factor of 0.233 kg CO₂ per kWh, based on the average grid mix.

Diet

Different diets have different carbon impacts due to production methods and resource use:

  • Omnivore: 2.5 kg CO₂ per day
  • Vegetarian: 1.7 kg CO₂ per day
  • Vegan: 1.5 kg CO₂ per day
  • Pescatarian: 2.0 kg CO₂ per day
